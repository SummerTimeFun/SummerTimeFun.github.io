About Me

Hello! I'm Summer. I'm a Brand & Product Designer with a keen interest in research. I'm skilled at branding, wireframing, prototyping, mobile design and talking to humans. I am super jazzed about Speculative Design and Sci-Fi.

I've been a user-centric designer for several years, starting in Interior Design. In Interior Design, I focused on creating accessible washroom spaces that didn't look institutionalized.

After an injury, I had to leave the field, eventually bringing me to Brand Design via UX/UIU. Like any good designer, I have a winding path full of exciting experiences. I got my start in tech by learning to code. When I heard the term UX Designer, I knew that was the direction I needed to head. My odd combination of psychology and design came together for the first time. And it fulfilled my work-life goals to utilize my natural design talents to create better experiences. Huzzah!!!
