I'm Summer.

As a UX designer with a background in Interior Design, I have a strong understanding of the design process and how to build things for humans. I love creating better experiences for people through creative problem-solving. I'm also pretty keen on sci-fi and future design.

Projects

  • Native Mobile App

    Onfleet is the trusted last mile delivery solution for thousands of companies across dozens of industries including food and beverage, retail, e-commerce, furniture, pharmacy and more. The SaaS was a dashboard for Dispatchers and a Drivers app on Android and iOS. I was the Product Designer on the mobile squad improving the experience for drivers.

  • Service Design

    After observing a significant gap in my organization's service blueprint, I set about to redesign the entire support process. I created a multi-tiered support system that included the implementation of an internal-use dashboard, educational materials and documentation.

    Read Case Study
  • GroJo

    Houseplants are increasing in popularity everyday. Often, people aren't sure how much and when to water their little green fam. GroJo helps take the second guessing out of house plant care so you can enjoy your greener home with ease. This project highlights my adaptive abilites.

    Read Case Study
  • Here To Slay

    The STEAM/STEM industry is one of the fastest-growing, and yet there is still a lack of women and non-binary people in leadership roles. This project applies design thinking through divergence and convergence to explore this wicked problem.

    Read Case Study
  • Re-Feed

    Food waste is one of the most economical and environmental issues we are currently facing. The amount of waste produced in the production and destruction of food has a serious impact on climate change. It is also a major struggle for most people to get a handle on in their personal lives. Re-Feed looks at ways to end the shame cycle and reduce food waste while creating community.
